Evidence-Based Approach

Research Overview

IMWIRSA's research work sits between two roles: gathering independent evidence about seafarer welfare, and translating outside academic and institutional research into practical product and policy decisions. Every feature in MWApp and every recommendation in our White Paper traces back to a data point, a study, or a seafarer survey.

🔬

Independent studies

Our own surveys and data collection directly with seafarers and Port Welfare Managers.

📚

Curated external research

We track and synthesise the leading academic and institutional literature on maritime welfare.

💡

Practical translation

Findings become concrete recommendations for industry, policy and product design.

Our Approach

From independent research to measurably better welfare — a transparent, evidence-driven process.
