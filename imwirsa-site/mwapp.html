MWApp Overview

One app, everything a port arrival needs

MWApp is IMWIRSA's flagship product: a progressive web app (PWA) — meaning it runs straight in the browser, with no app-store install and no account to create. Open it, choose a language and an assistant, and it already knows which port you're in.

🗺️

Live Port Card

Transport, seafarers' centre, medical, safety — curated locally, not crowdsourced.

🧑‍💼

A real local coordinator

Every port card is maintained by a Port Welfare Manager who actually lives there.

💬

24/7 volunteer line

Anonymous, live, human — for anything from directions to a crisis.

The Case For MWApp

Why MWApp?

Because right now, finding real help in an unfamiliar port depends on luck — who you ask, whether they speak your language, whether the info is even current.

⏱️

Port stays keep shrinking

Turnaround times are falling. A seafarer with two hours ashore can't afford to search.

🌍

One tool, every port

The same app, the same logic, wherever the ship calls next — not a different flyer each time.

🗣️

In your language

English, Russian, Turkish and Filipino at MVP launch, chosen from real crew demographics.

🔒

No install, no friction

A PWA works instantly on any phone, over patchy port wifi, with no app-store account.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Human behind the data

Every port card has a named, contactable coordinator — not an anonymous database.

📊

Built on evidence

Designed around IMWIRSA's own research into what seafarers actually need on arrival.

The Core Data Layer

Port Cards

Every pilot port gets a living "card": structured, always-current information across five categories, maintained by the local Port Welfare Manager.

🚌

Transport

Shuttles, taxis, walking routes and distances from the terminal gate.

🏛

Seafarers' Centre

Opening hours, services, contact and directions to the local centre or mission.

🛍️

Shops & Essentials

SIM cards, currency exchange, groceries, and what's actually open at 2 AM.

🩺

Medical

Pharmacies, clinics, and where English (or your language) is spoken.

🚨

Safety

Local emergency numbers, port security contacts and area risk notes.

MWA — Maritime Welfare Assistant

Meet the AI Assistants

On first launch, a seafarer chooses the assistant who feels right for them. That choice — and only that choice — shapes their entire MWApp experience.

🧑
Alex
General / European audience

A young, neutral, approachable presence for a broad international crew.

👨
Omar
Arabic, Turkish, Middle Eastern audience

An older, steady voice for seafarers who want a familiar, respectful tone.

👩
Sophia
European audience

A young, warm female voice — the default guide for many first-time users.

🧕
Amina
Muslim audience, any country

A respectful, culturally attuned presence for Muslim seafarers worldwide.

The assistant remembers the seafarer's name (with consent), greets them personally on return visits, and hands off smoothly to the local Port Welfare Manager or a dedicated Wellness Assistant when a situation calls for it. Changing assistant or language is always available in Settings.

Behind Every Port Card

Port Welfare Manager — Telegram Bot

Every port card is kept current by a real local coordinator — the Port Welfare Manager (PWM) — through a simple Telegram bot, no separate app or dashboard to learn.

📝

Guided onboarding

A 15–25 minute registration walks the PWM through 9 sections covering their details, the port and its services.

🔄

One-tap updates

Change opening hours, transport, or add an urgent notice in seconds, right from the chat.

🚨

Urgent notifications

Push a time-sensitive alert straight to every seafarer currently in port.

📊

Simple analytics

See how many seafarers viewed the port card and used each service — no separate reporting tool.

What's Next

Future Development

MWApp launches as an MVP focused on getting the fundamentals right. Here's what's planned as the network grows.

👪

Family support

A dedicated space to help seafarers' families stay connected and supported ashore.

⚕️

Telemedicine

Online medical and legal consultations, integrated directly into the app.

🪪

Union card access

Priority services and the Wellness Recovery Zone unlocked through union membership.

🌐

50+ ports by 2032

Scaling the platform well beyond the five Phase 1 pilot ports.

🗣️

More languages

Expanding past the four MVP languages as the crew base of partner ports grows.

📈

Continuous feedback

A permanent channel for seafarers to shape what MWApp builds next.
